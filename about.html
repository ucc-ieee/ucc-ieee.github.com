Who are we?

We are students member of the IEEE in University College Cork. The IEEE or Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is a non-profit professional association headquartered in New York City that is dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence.

Constiution for University College Cork IEEE Student Chapter

Name: University College Cork (UCC) IEEE Student Chapter Ireland

Purpose

Encourage and empower the student community at UCC to engage in electrical and electronic engineering activities for personal and professional development and to positively contribute on local, national, and international levels through student activities.

Sec. 1 This organization shall be known as the University College Cork Branch of the IEEE.

Sec. 2 The purpose shall be the dissemination of knowledge of the theory and practice of all aspects of electrical engineering, electronics, radio, allied branches of engineering or the related arts and sciences, as well as the furtherance of the professional development of the Students.

Sec. 3 The organization and operation of the Branch shall be in accordance with the Constitution and Bylaws of the IEEE.

Membership

Sec. 4 Membership shall be limited to undergraduate and graduate students of University College Cork.

Sec. 5 The membership shall consist of Student members of the IEEE.

Officers

Sec. 6. The officers of the Branch shall be a Chairperson, a Vice-Chairperson, a Treasurer, an Events Officer, a Public Relations Officer and two Ordinary Committee Members.

Sec. 7 To be eligible for office, a candidate must be enrolled as a Student member of the IEEE.

Sec. 8 The term of office shall ordinarily be one year.

Sec. 9 Election of officers for the following year shall be held not later than the last meeting of each year.

Executive Committee

Sec. 10 The management of the affairs of the Branch shall be in the hands of the Executive Committee, consisting of the duly elected student officers of the Branch and the EEE Department Head appointed Academic Faculty Advisor for the Branch. Daily operations, meetings and decisions will be managed by the Student management of the Branch. Branch level executive decisions will be taken in consultation and approval of the Faculty Advisor.

Duties of Officers

Sec. 11 The Chairperson shall preside at all meetings of the Branch. He/she shall appoint all committees, subject to the approval of the Executive Committee, and shall assume all other executive duties not otherwise delegated. The Chairperson shall carry on all other communications necessary to the activities of the Branch. The Chairperson shall keep a record of the names of members in attendance at the meetings.

Sec. 12 The Vice-Chairperson shall perform all functions of the Chairperson in the latter's absence or at his/her request. The Vice Chairperson shall keep a record of all activities of the Branch and shall report same to IEEE Headquarters, together with any special reports required by IEEE Headquarters, within a reasonable time after each meeting.

Sec. 13 The Treasurer shall receive all money and pay all debts of the Branch authorized by the Executive Committee, and shall keep an exact account of all receipts and expenditures.

Sec. 14 A complete annual report shall be submitted by the Chairperson and the Treasurer at the last regular meeting of the year, and shall be forwarded by the Branch Vice Chairperson to the IEEE Manager of Student Services, upon certification by the Academic Advisor.

Meetings

Sec. 15 The Branch shall hold regular and special meetings at such places and time as designated by the Executive Committee, with a minimum of 2 meetings a year.

Amendments

Sec. 16 The Branch is empowered to adopt Bylaws which are consistent with this Constitution.

Sec. 17 The Constitution may be amended by a two-thirds of the members of the Branch present and voting, provided the amendment shall have been proposed at least one duly publicized meeting previous to the time of voting. Such amendments become effective only upon the approval of the Executive Committee of the IEEE.
